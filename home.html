Our Story


ON DECK Store is the leading online skate shop, providing a large selection of decks, wheels, bearings, trucks and skateboard completes. With our store located in Beirut, Roum Hospital Street, face Guilbert Store, we provide service to local customers as well. We carry sneakers, longboards and pennyboards from the best brands such as Nike SB, Vans, Grizzly, Carpet, Santa Cruiz and many. If you're looking to create a custom masterpiece, go to our shop and we'll handle things.
